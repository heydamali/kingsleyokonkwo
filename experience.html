  • Engine Systems

    Co-Founder & Product Lead

    Jan 2020 - Nov 2021

Having worked remotely as engineers for years, my co-founder and I saw an opportunity to help businesses build the tools they needed. We grew from a two-person team to seven engineers and a product designer in under two years, and I led the product development effort across clients in the US and Nigeria.

For Stir Money, I oversaw the development of a dashboard that solved fragmented income visibility for creators by unifying earnings across platforms through integrations with 20+ fintech APIs. For Gigster, I led the build of a talent matching portal with integrated project management features that streamlined their hiring and delivery process. We also built tools for several local businesses in Nigeria.

    I ultimately left to explore crypto's potential to expand access to global financial systems in emerging economies .

  • Generation Enterprise
