Kingsley Okonkwo

  • BIO

    Kingsley manages digital products at the intersection of finance and technology, with a focus on expanding access to global financial systems in emerging economies. He is currently pursuing his MBA at UC Berkeley Haas, learning how to scale products globally, navigate complex regulatory environments, and build businesses that create lasting impact in underserved markets.

    Before business school, he was a Technical Program Manager on the Developer Platform team at MetaMask (Consensys), where he built tools used by thousands of businesses to integrate with decentralized financial infrastructure. His work spanned APIs, SDKs, and unified platforms that enable companies participate in the open financial system.

    Kingsley started his career as a software engineer before moving into Developer Relations, giving him a technical foundation that shapes how he works with engineering teams and builds products developers actually want to use.
